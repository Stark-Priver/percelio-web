# 🎬 Video Script — Testimonial Video

**Title:** "Why Mbeya Loves Pacelio"  
**Length:** 45–60 seconds  
**Style:** Documentary-style, real people, authentic  
**Tone:** Warm, genuine, community-focused

---

## Script

**0:00–0:08** — Mwanaisha (Shop Owner, Soweto Market)  
*On camera, in her shop, holding a package*

"Before Pacelio, I used to call three or four riders every time I needed a delivery. I never knew if they'd show up, or when they'd arrive, or if my customer actually received the package."

**0:08–0:18** — B-roll: Mwanaisha using Pacelio app on her phone, smiling

"Now I book everything on Pacelio. It takes seconds. My customers get tracking links. They love it. I love it."

**0:18–0:28** — Juma (Courier)  
*On camera, next to his motorcycle*

"I used to wait at the stage all day hoping for work. Now jobs come to my phone. I earn more, I save fuel with the GPS routes, and I have freedom."

**0:28–0:38** — B-roll: Juma accepting a delivery on the app, checking the route, riding off

"My best day? TZS 45,000. I never made that waiting at the stage."

**0:38–0:48** — Hadija (Restaurant Owner)  
*On camera, handing a food order to a courier*

"Our delivery orders have gone up 30% since we started using Pacelio. Customers trust the tracking. We trust the system. It's a win-win."

**0:48–0:60** — Group shot: Mwanaisha, Juma, Hadija, and the Pacelio team  
*Text appears: "Pacelio. Built in Mbeya. For East Africa."*

"Pacelio is building something special."  
"Smarter deliveries for growing businesses."  
"This is just the beginning." 🚀
