# 🎬 Video Script — Behind the Scenes: Building Pacelio

**Title:** "Building Pacelio — From a University Project to a Logistics Platform"  
**Length:** 2–3 minutes  
**Style:** Documentary / vlog, authentic, raw  
**Tone:** Inspiring, personal, real

---

## Script

**0:00–0:15** — Establishing shot: Mbeya University of Science and Technology campus  
*Text: "Mbeya University of Science and Technology — January 2026"*

**VO:** "This is where it all started. Two students, a diploma project, and an idea that grew way bigger than a classroom."

---

**0:15–0:35** — Privatus and Peter at a desk, coding. Quick cuts of code on screen, phones buzzing, coffee cups.

**VO:** "Privatus and Peter noticed something: deliveries in Mbeya were broken. Phone calls. No tracking. Cash-only. They built a simple prototype for their final year project."

---

**0:35–0:55** — Street interviews / B-roll of Mbeya streets, markets, motorcycle stages.

**VO:** "But when they showed local businesses, the response was immediate. A restaurant in Iyunga. A boutique in Soweto. A hardware store in Mabatini. They all wanted in."

---

**0:55–1:20** — Team in IncriprTech office, whiteboard with planning, laughing, working together.

**VO:** "They turned their project into a company. They built a team. They built a platform. Not an imported solution — something designed for Tanzania."

---

**1:20–1:50** — Montage of couriers delivering, app screens, businesses using the platform, happy customers.

**VO:** "In June 2026, Pacelio went live. 50 couriers. 120 businesses. 20 wards across Mbeya. Real-time tracking. QR handoffs. Automated COD. Real deliveries, real impact."

---

**1:50–2:15** — Map animation showing expansion dots: Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Mwanza, Dodoma, Nairobi, Kampala.

**VO:** "This is just the beginning. We're expanding across Tanzania, then across borders. The logistics backbone of East Africa is being built — right here in Mbeya."

---

**2:15–2:30** — Closing shot: Privatus, Peter, and team standing together, sunset, Mbeya skyline.

**Text:** "Pacelio. Built in Mbeya. For East Africa."  
**Logo + Website:** app.pacelio.incpritech.com

**VO:** "World-class logistics can start anywhere. Even at a university desk."

---
