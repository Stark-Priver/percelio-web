# 🎬 Video Script — 60-Second Explainer Video

**Title:** "What is Pacelio?"  
**Length:** 60 seconds  
**Style:** Animated / motion graphics  
**Tone:** Clear, exciting, professional  
**Music:** Upbeat, modern, Afro-optimistic

---

| Time | Visual | Audio / Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| **0:00–0:05** | Animated map of East Africa with delivery routes crisscrossing. Orange Pacelio logo appears center. | "East Africa is moving. Goods flowing. Businesses growing. But logistics is stuck in the past." |
| **0:05–0:12** | Split screen: Left shows chaotic old way (phone calls, notebooks, confused looks). Right goes dark. | "Phone calls. Handwritten records. No tracking. It's slow, unreliable, and expensive." |
| **0:12–0:18** | Right side lights up with clean Pacelio UI. Left side fades. Phone screen showing booking flow. | "Introducing Pacelio. The smart way to send and receive packages across East Africa." |
| **0:18–0:25** | Animated steps: 1. Book (tap phone), 2. Match (courier icon moves), 3. Track (map with GPS dot), 4. Deliver (QR scan, checkmark). | "Book in seconds. Auto-matched to the best courier. Track every step in real time. QR-secured proof of delivery." |
| **0:25–0:32** | Split into 3 panels: Business owner (smiling at dashboard), Courier (on bike, money icon), User (receiving package). | "For businesses. For couriers. For everyone. One platform, endless possibilities." |
| **0:32–0:40** | Stats appear one by one on screen: 3,000+ couriers, 120+ businesses, 20 wards, TZS 47.8T market. | "Thousands of couriers. Hundreds of businesses. A multi-trillion shilling market. And we're just getting started." |
| **0:40–0:50** | Map zooming out from Mbeya to show Tanzania, then East Africa. Dots appearing in Dar, Arusha, Mwanza, Nairobi, Kampala. | "Launched in Mbeya. Expanding across Tanzania. Coming soon to Kenya and Uganda." |
| **0:50–0:60** | Pacelio logo + "app.pacelio.incpritech.com" on screen. Phone mockup showing the app. Orange background. | "Pacelio. Smarter deliveries for growing businesses. Try it today." |
