# 🎬 Video Script — 90-Second Product Demo

**Title:** "How Pacelio Works — Full Demo"  
**Length:** 90 seconds  
**Style:** Screen recording + face-to-camera cutaways  
**Tone:** Educational, clear, helpful

---

| Time | Visual | Audio |
|---|---|---|
| **0:00–0:08** | Host on camera, standing in a real Mbeya street. "I'm at a local shop in Mbeya." | "I'm at Mama Nasiim's shop in Soweto. She needs to send a package to a customer in Iyunga. Let me show you how easy it is with Pacelio." |
| **0:08–0:15** | Screen recording: Opening Pacelio web app. Login screen → Dashboard. | "I'll open the Pacelio web app on my phone. If you're new, signup takes less than a minute." |
| **0:15–0:30** | Screen recording: Booking flow. Enter pickup (Soweto Market), delivery (Iyunga), package size → "See available couriers" button. | "I enter the pickup address — Mama's shop in Soweto. Delivery goes to Iyunga. Package is small. Now I tap 'See available couriers'..." |
| **0:30–0:40** | Screen recording: Results showing 3 couriers with prices TZS 2,500 / 3,000 / 3,500 and estimated times. | "Three couriers are available right now. Prices range from TZS 2,500 to TZS 3,500. And the estimated delivery time is 25–35 minutes. I'll pick the middle option." |
| **0:40–0:50** | Screen recording: Booking confirmed. Map view showing courier's real-time location. | "Booked. Now look — I can see the courier's location in real time. I'm sharing this tracking link with Mama Nasiim so she can follow along." |
| **0:50–1:00** | Host on camera showing the package being handed to courier. | "The courier arrives. He scans the package. It's officially in the system. Now both Mama and her customer can track it live." |
| **1:00–1:10** | Screen within screen: Customer receiving delivery in Iyunga. QR scan on courier's phone. | "30 minutes later, the courier arrives at the customer. She scans her QR code — and the delivery is complete with proof." |
| **1:10–1:20** | Screen recording: Dashboard showing "Delivery Complete" with timestamp, rating screen. | "Back on the dashboard, the delivery shows as complete. Mama can rate the courier and see the full delivery history." |
| **1:20–1:30** | Host on camera at shop. | "That's it. From booking to delivery in under 30 minutes. No phone calls. No confusion. Just reliable, trackable delivery." |

---

## Production Notes

| Element | Detail |
