## PACELIO RAISES $XXX IN PRE-SEED FUNDING TO SCALE EAST AFRICAN LOGISTICS PLATFORM

*Funding will accelerate expansion from Mbeya to Tanzania's top 6 cities and cross-border operations*

**MBEYA, TANZANIA** — Pacelio, East Africa's multi-carrier courier and logistics marketplace, today announced it has raised $XXX in pre-seed funding from {{Investor Name / Group}}.

The investment will be used to scale Pacelio's platform from its current operations in Mbeya to Tanzania's five largest cities — Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Mwanza, Dodoma, and Zanzibar — and to begin cross-border pilot operations with Kenya.

Pacelio launched in Mbeya in June 2026 and has since onboarded 50+ couriers and 120+ business partners. The platform processes thousands of deliveries monthly with real-time GPS tracking, QR-secured handoffs, and automated COD management.

"East African logistics is at an inflection point," said Privatus Cosmas, Co-Founder of Pacelio. "Cross-border e-commerce is exploding, urbanization is accelerating, but 65% of the logistics market still operates offline. This funding allows us to bridge that gap faster."

The platform addresses a TZS 47.8 trillion (~$19 billion) market growing at 15% CAGR. With 70%+ mobile money penetration and a rapidly digitizing SME sector, Tanzania represents a significant opportunity for digital logistics infrastructure.

"Our investment in Pacelio reflects our conviction that the next wave of African innovation will come from local founders solving local problems," said {{Investor Quote}}.

Pacelio's six revenue streams include delivery commissions, business subscriptions, premium courier listings, API licensing, SMS services, and analytics. The company projects revenue of TZS 420M in its first year.

**About Pacelio:**
Pacelio connects businesses, couriers, logistics companies, and individual users across East Africa through a single digital marketplace. Built by Tanzanian developers and headquartered in Mbeya, the platform offers real-time tracking, multi-carrier rate comparison, QR handoffs, and COD management. Visit pacelio.incpritech.com.
