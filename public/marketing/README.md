## 🎨 Using These Files

Each `.md` file is designed to be:
1. **Read directly** for campaign inspiration
2. **Given to AI tools** (image generators, content writers, video creators) as precise instructions
3. **Adapted** for your specific channel and audience

**Pro Tip:** For AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion), copy the "Visual" descriptions from flyers and social posts as prompts.
