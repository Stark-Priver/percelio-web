## Post 3: Why Local Matters

**Format:** Text only  
**Best for:** Investors, partners, ecosystem builders

**Headline:** The most important decision we made: building for East Africa, by East Africans.

**Body:**

When we started Pacelio, we had a choice:
→ Adapt an existing logistics platform from outside Africa
→ Build something from scratch, designed for our market

We chose the second path. Here's why:

🇹🇿 **Mobile money is king** — 70%+ of Tanzanian adults use mobile money. Our platform is built around M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, and Airtel Money integration — not credit cards.

📱 **Mobile-first, always** — Most users will never touch a desktop. Our entire platform works beautifully on a smartphone.

🗣️ **Swahili matters** — Not everyone speaks English. Our platform works in both English and Swahili, and we're adding more local languages.

💰 **COD is not a bug, it's a feature** — Cash on delivery accounts for 60% of transactions. We built end-to-end COD management, not a workaround.

📶 **Offline-first thinking** — Internet isn't always reliable. We design for intermittent connectivity.

The global logistics playbooks don't work here. We wrote our own.

That's our superpower.
