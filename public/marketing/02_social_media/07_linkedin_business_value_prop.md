## Post 2: Why Customer Trust Depends on Tracking

**Format:** Text  
**Target:** Business owners, marketing managers

**Headline:** Your customers care about one thing more than delivery speed.

**Body:**

It's not speed. It's certainty.

Research shows that customers value knowing WHEN their package will arrive over it arriving fast.

A delivery that arrives in 2 hours with no tracking creates more anxiety than a delivery that arrives in 4 hours with real-time updates.

Why? Because uncertainty is worse than waiting.

When your customers can see exactly where their package is:
→ They stop calling your support line
→ They trust your business more
→ They're more likely to order again

Pacelio gives every delivery its own tracking link. Shareable. Real-time. Accurate.

Stop making your customers guess. Give them certainty.
