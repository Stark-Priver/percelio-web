## Slide 4: How It Works

```
Business/User books → Auto-matched to nearest courier → Real-time GPS tracking → QR proof of delivery → Automated payment
```

1. **Book** — Enter pickup/delivery details in seconds
2. **Match** — AI matches to the best available courier
3. **Track** — Live GPS tracking from pickup to drop-off
4. **Handoff** — Secure QR code verification at delivery
5. **Pay** — Cash on Delivery or mobile money, auto-reconciled
