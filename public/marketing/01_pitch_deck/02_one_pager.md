# 📄 Pacelio — One-Pager

**Pacelio** is East Africa's multi-carrier courier and logistics platform. We connect businesses, individuals, courier agents, and logistics companies through a single digital marketplace — making delivery fast, trackable, and reliable.

---

## The Problem
65% of East Africa's logistics market has no digital solution. Businesses rely on phone calls, cash-only transactions, and hope. No tracking. No proof of delivery. No accountability.

## The Solution
A single platform where:
- **Businesses** book deliveries in seconds, compare rates, and track in real time
- **Couriers** receive instant job alerts, optimized routes, and instant payouts
- **Logistics companies** expand coverage without infrastructure investment
- **Users** send parcels affordably with full visibility

## Key Metrics
- 📦 **3,000+** couriers on platform
- 📍 **20 wards** covered in Mbeya
- 🏪 **120+** partner businesses
- 📊 **TZS 47.8T** East Africa logistics market
- 📈 **15%** CAGR (2026–2030)

## Revenue Model
Commission per delivery + Tiered subscriptions + Premium features + API licensing

## The Ask
**$50K – $100K** pre-seed to scale from Mbeya to 6 Tanzanian cities, onboard 1,000+ couriers, and launch cross-border pilot.
