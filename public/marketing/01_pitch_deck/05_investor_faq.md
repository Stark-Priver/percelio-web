# ❓ Pacelio — Investor FAQ

## General

**Q: What is Pacelio?**  
A: A multi-carrier courier and logistics marketplace connecting businesses, couriers, logistics companies, and individual users across East Africa.

**Q: Where are you based?**  
A: Mbeya, Tanzania. We serve Mbeya and expanding to Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Mwanza, and Dodoma.

**Q: Who is behind Pacelio?**  
A: Privatus Cosmas and Peter Ntonda, Final Year Diploma students at Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST), backed by IncriprTech.

---

## Market

**Q: How big is the opportunity?**  
A: TZS 47.8 trillion East African courier and logistics market, growing at 15% CAGR. Only 35% has any digital solution.

**Q: Who are your competitors?**  
A: Traditional courier services operate offline with phone-based coordination. Regional players are single-carrier only. No platform offers multi-carrier comparison, real-time tracking, QR handoffs, and COD management in one app.

**Q: Why Tanzania first?**  
A: Tanzania is the gateway to East Africa — stable economy, 70%+ mobile money penetration, rapidly urbanizing population, and a massive SME sector underserved by logistics.

---

## Business Model

**Q: How do you make money?**  
A: Six streams: delivery commission (5–15%), business subscriptions, premium courier listings, SMS notifications, API licensing, and premium analytics.

**Q: What are your unit economics?**  
A: Avg TZS 3,500 per delivery, 10% blended commission, TZS 15,000 CAC (targeting TZS 5,000), TZS 280K LTV (targeting TZS 850K).

**Q: When will you be profitable?**  
A: Projected profitability by mid-Year 2 as we hit scale in 6 cities.

---

## Product

**Q: What makes your tech different?**  
A: Single codebase, role-based dashboards (business, courier, logistics, user), AI route optimization, QR-secured handoffs, real-time GPS tracking, multi-language (English & Swahili), and offline-capable features.

**Q: Do you have an app?**  
A: Yes — iOS and Android (Coming Soon). Web at app.pacelio.incpritech.com.

**Q: How do you handle COD?**  
A: End-to-end digital COD management — tracking, collection, reconciliation, and automated payouts to businesses.

---

## Competition

**Q: What is your moat?**  
A: Local knowledge (built by Tanzanians for East Africa), dual-language, deep understanding of informal logistics networks and mobile money culture, multi-carrier marketplace model, and first-mover advantage in secondary cities.

**Q: How do you attract couriers?**  
A: Instant job alerts, optimized routes, daily/weekly payouts, performance bonuses, and flexible scheduling.

## Impact

**Q: What is your social impact?**  
A: Creating formal employment for thousands of couriers, enabling SMEs to access reliable logistics for the first time, reducing transportation costs for consumers, and contributing to formalization of the informal economy.

**Q: How do you measure impact?**  
A: Couriers onboarded & earnings, businesses served, deliveries completed (especially in underserved areas), digital payment adoption, and reduction in delivery times.
