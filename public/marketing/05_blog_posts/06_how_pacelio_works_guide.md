## The Complete Guide to Sending a Package with Pacelio

Never used a delivery app before? No problem. Here's exactly how to send a package with Pacelio — from start to finish.

**What You'll Need:**
- A smartphone with internet (or a computer)
- The package you want to send
- The delivery address
- Mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money) or cash

**Step 1: Open Pacelio**
Go to app.pacelio.incpritech.com on your phone or computer. If it's your first time, tap "Sign Up" and enter your name, phone number, and email. It takes about 60 seconds.

**Step 2: Enter Delivery Details**
Tap "New Delivery." Enter:
- **Pickup address** — where the package is now
- **Delivery address** — where it needs to go
- **Package description** — what's inside, approximate size
- **Special instructions** — anything the courier should know

**Step 3: Compare & Choose a Courier**
You'll see a list of available couriers near the pickup location. Each shows:
- **Price** — how much the delivery costs
- **Estimated time** — how long it will take
- **Rating** — what other users think of this courier

Pick the one that works best for you.

**Step 4: Track Your Delivery**
Once you book, a courier is dispatched. You can watch their location on a live map — from pickup to drop-off. Share the tracking link with the person receiving the package so they can follow along too.

**Step 5: Receive Confirmation**
When the package is delivered, the receiver scans a QR code on the courier's phone. You get a notification: "Delivery Complete." You can rate the courier and view proof of delivery.

**Step 6: Payment**
If you chose Cash on Delivery, the receiver pays the courier. If you pre-paid via mobile money, it's automatically processed.

**That's It.**
No phone calls. No guessing. No stress. Just reliable, trackable delivery.

**Pro Tips for First-Time Users:**
- 📸 Take a photo of the package before the courier picks it up
- 📱 Share the tracking link with your customer immediately
- ⭐ Rate your courier — it helps the whole community
- 📍 Save frequent addresses for faster booking next time
