## How Logistics Companies Benefit from the Pacelio Network

If you run a logistics company in Tanzania, you face a familiar challenge: your fleet is either overcapacity or underutilized. Peak periods strain your resources. Slow periods drain your margins.

Pacelio offers a new model: don't just own capacity — access it.

**The Capacity Problem**

Most logistics companies operate at 60–70% fleet utilization. During peak seasons (festivals, harvest seasons, holiday shopping), they turn away business. During slow periods, vehicles sit idle.

The traditional solution: buy more trucks. Hire more drivers. Invest more capital.

The smarter solution: integrate with Pacelio's courier network.

**How the Partnership Works**

Logistics companies connect to Pacelio in two ways:

**1. As a Capacity Provider**
Your existing fleet joins Pacelio's network. When nearby deliveries are available, your drivers receive job alerts. You set the pricing. You keep control. Pacelio handles the marketplace, tracking, and payments.

**2. As a Capacity Consumer**
During peak periods, you tap into Pacelio's network of 3,000+ couriers. Overflow deliveries are routed to available couriers automatically. Your customers get the same tracking experience. You pay per delivery — no fixed cost.
