## The Challenge

{{Company}} operates a fleet of 15 delivery vehicles serving Mbeya and surrounding areas. They faced a common problem: during peak hours (10 AM – 2 PM and 5 PM – 8 PM), demand exceeded capacity. They were turning away 20–30 deliveries per day.

The traditional solution — buy more vehicles — wasn't feasible. Vehicle purchase, driver hiring, and maintenance costs would take months to recoup.

"We needed capacity that could flex with demand. We couldn't justify buying 5 more trucks for peak hours that would sit idle the rest of the day."

---

## The Solution

{{Company}} partnered with Pacelio to access on-demand courier capacity.

**How It Works:**
1. When {{Company}}'s fleet is fully utilized, overflow deliveries are routed to Pacelio's courier network
2. Couriers receive job alerts, complete the delivery, and provide GPS tracking + QR proof
3. {{Company}} pays per delivery — no fixed cost, no minimum commitment
4. Customers experience the same tracking and quality regardless of who delivers
