# 📊 Case Study — Mama Nguo Fashion: 30% Sales Growth with Pacelio

**Business:** Mama Nguo Fashion — Women's Clothing Boutique  
**Location:** Soweto Market, Mbeya  
**Founded:** 2021  
**Size:** 2 employees  
**Pacelio Customer Since:** June 2026

---

## The Challenge

Mama Nguo Fashion sells women's clothing and accessories. Before Pacelio, deliveries were managed through a single motorcycle rider who charged a fixed TZS 5,000 per delivery — regardless of distance or package size.

**Pain Points:**
- No backup rider — if the rider was busy, deliveries waited
- No tracking — customers called constantly asking "where is my order?"
- COD was managed in a notebook — weekly reconciliation took 3+ hours
- Lost packages (3 in the previous month) with no proof of delivery
- Limited delivery area to customers within walking distance

---

## The Solution

Mama Nguo signed up for Pacelio's free business account and started using the platform for all deliveries.

**Key Features Used:**
- Multi-carrier rate comparison (average cost dropped to TZS 3,200)
- Real-time GPS tracking
- QR-secured proof of delivery
- Automated COD reconciliation
- Delivery dashboard with analytics

---

## The Results

| Metric | Before Pacelio | After Pacelio | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Avg Delivery Cost** | TZS 5,000 | TZS 3,200 | **-36%** |
| **Monthly Deliveries** | ~60 | ~95 | **+58%** |
| **Delivery Area** | Walking distance | All 20 wards of Mbeya | **5x expansion** |
| **COD Reconciliation Time** | 3+ hours/week | 20 minutes/week | **-89%** |
| **Lost Package Disputes** | 3/month | 0/month | **-100%** |
| **Customer Complaints** | 12–15/month | 1–2/month | **-87%** |
| **Monthly Sales** | TZS 4.2M | TZS 5.5M | **+31%** |

## Key Takeaways

1. **Multi-carrier competition** reduced delivery costs by 36%
2. **Larger delivery area** unlocked 25% new customers from other wards
3. **Real-time tracking** eliminated customer anxiety and reduced support calls
4. **Automated COD** freed 3+ hours per week for business growth activities
5. **QR proof of delivery** eliminated disputes entirely
