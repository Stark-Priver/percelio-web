## Platform-Specific Strategy

### Instagram
- **Primary:** #Pacelio + 5 niche hashtags
- **Mix:** 3 broad + 3 specific + location tags
- **Placement:** In first comment (cleaner caption)
- **Stories:** Location stickers, poll stickers, question box

### LinkedIn
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 professional hashtags in post body
- **Top picks:** #Logistics #SupplyChain #EastAfrica #Tanzania #TechForGood #LastMileDelivery
- **No hashtag stuffing** — LinkedIn algorithm prefers quality over quantity

### Twitter/X
- **Hashtags:** 1–2 max per tweet
- **Focus:** Trending topics when relevant
- **Threads:** No hashtags in thread, only in first/last tweet

### TikTok
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 trending + niche
- **Sound:** Use trending sounds with relevant overlay text
- **Format:** #Pacelio in caption, relevant trend hashtags

### Facebook
- **Hashtags:** 2–3 max per post
- **Strategy:** Focus on local community groups, marketplace, business pages
