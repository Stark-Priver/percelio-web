# 🎨 Pacelio Brand Guidelines Summary

**Version:** 1.0 | **Last Updated:** July 2026

## Brand Identity

**Mission:** To make delivery reliable, trackable, and affordable for every business and person in East Africa.

**Vision:** The digital backbone of East African logistics.

**Values:**
- 🇹🇿 **Local First** — Solutions designed for our market, by our people
- 🔍 **Transparency** — Real-time tracking, clear pricing, no surprises
- 🤝 **Trust** — QR-secured handoffs, verified couriers, reliable service
- 📈 **Growth** — Empowering businesses and couriers to thrive

## Typography

**Primary Font:** Inter (Sans-serif)
- Headlines: Bold / Extra Bold
- Body: Regular / Medium
- UI Elements: Medium / Semi Bold

**Fallback:** Helvetica Neue, Arial, sans-serif

```
Headline 1: Bold, 48px, -0.5 tracking
Headline 2: Bold, 36px, -0.3 tracking
Headline 3: Bold, 24px, normal tracking
Body: Regular, 16px, 1.6 line height
Small: Regular, 14px
Caption: Regular, 12px
```

## Logo Usage

### Clear Space
Minimum clear space = 1x logo height on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 32px height minimum
- Print: 15mm height minimum

### Logo Variations
1. **Primary** — Orange icon + "Pacelio" wordmark (default)
2. **White** — White icon + wordmark for dark backgrounds
3. **Icon Only** — Orange shipping icon (favicon, app icon, social avatar)

### Don'ts
❌ Stretch or distort
❌ Change colors
❌ Add drop shadows or effects
❌ Place on busy/noisy backgrounds
❌ Rearrange icon and wordmark
❌ Use on similar-colored backgrounds

---

## Imagery Style

- **Photography:** Real people, real locations, authentic moments
- **Illustration:** Simple, clean, orange-accented line art
- **Icons:** Consistent outline style, rounded corners
- **Colors:** Warm, vibrant, optimistic
- **Subjects:** Business owners, couriers, customers, packages, cityscapes
- **Avoid:** Stock photography, overly staged scenes, irrelevant locations
